Paperlib

Paperlib automatically organizes your scanned documents and keeps track of your paid and unpaid invoices, so that the minute you want to pay your bills, you can. It's better than having them lay around on your hard drive, clutering your desktop, or in some (not so safe) cloud.

It lets you search and instantly find the document you need, because it knows you are specifically looking for invoices (or contracts *) from your insurance company (or telco, or dentist or else), and not for products or other irrevelant information about them.

Paperlib is currently in beta, but I'd be so happy to keep you posted on the updates (your email won't be used for anything else.)
